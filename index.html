Frequently Asked Questions

Get to know more about our courses

Q. What is "Tufquestions"?

"Tuf Questions" is a social brand engaged in offering high quality education with the motto "No learner should be denied an opportunity to quality education due to financial struggles." Currently, we are engaged in offering our services in entrance exam preparation domain.

Q. What is the mode of teaching?

All classes shall be held through live classes online through zoom or google meet.

Q. What is the class schedule and timings?

Classes shall be held 3 days in a week, 60 - 90 minutes per class. All classes shall be held either between 5 am to 7.30 am or 8 pm to 11 pm Indian time.

Q. What is the course fee?

Course fee shall be INR 5000 per year per student. Since we believe everyone should get opportunity to access high quality education, we do not have any intention to change this fee structure now or in future.

Q. How about the course content?

Course content shall be as per the syllabus prescribed by respective entrance conducting authorities. And our course is desigend to offer right amount of exposure to problem solving as well as theory, as required by the examinations.

Q. Do you conduct revision or doubt classes as well?

We conduct monthly revision classes as well as weekly doubt sessions to support IIT JEE and NEET aspirants.
