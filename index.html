Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is "Tufquestions"?

"Tuf Questions" is a test preparation initiative engaged in offering rigorous practice as well as exam grade mock tests to aid a student in their preparation towards dream education and career.

Q. What is free workshop concept?

Free Workshop Sessions are live streamed sessions, generally through Zoom, wherein the student can interact with our expert teachers directly. These workshops are held once in a month.

Q. What is the workshop schedule and timings?

Please register with us through join now form below to get notified of the free workshop schedule and timings.

Q. What is the course fee?

Live Workshops are free of cost and are streamed generally through zoom sessions. For pricing details on assessments, please click here.

Q. What kind of tests are included in weekly mock tests?

The type of tests depend on the classes that a student has enrolled for. For instance, for school exams, the tests shall be subjective in nature whereas for entrance preparation classes, it will be an objective MCQ test.Once the participant completes the weekly mock tests, they will receive the graded paper (wherever applicable).

To note, the student can choose the subject and chapter in which they would like to take the test.

Q. Do you conduct doubt resolution classes as well?

Every paid student would get free access to live weekly doubt resolution classes.
